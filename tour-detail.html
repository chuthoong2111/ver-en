Explore Amboseli & Chyulu Hills

Lying on the Tanzanian border, Amboseli National Park is renowned for its elephant herds, sweeping vistas, dusty red sunsets and, unrivalled views of the majestic Mount Kilimanjaro. The permanent swamps and lakes provide a range of habitats for diverse wildlife sightings. Just northeast of Amboseli lies the Chyulu Hills, a sprawling chain of mountains phenomenal in their natural beauty.

Highlights

  • Unrivalled views of Mount Kilimanjaro
  • Witness iconic large elephant herds
  • One of the world's largest lava tubes at Chyulu Hills
  • Exceptional bird watching and flora biodiversity
  • Elusive wildlife sightings including leopard and African wild dog

Iconic Activities

  • 4WD Safari
  • Guided Walking Safari
  • Night Drive
  • Cultural Experience
